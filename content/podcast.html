Welcome to The Sigmacast!

The Sigmacast is an audio podcast dedicated to content created by the Ergo community and the Ergo Foundation. Bringing you the audio from the latest videos published on the EF YouTube channel, such as the weekly AMAs and dev updates. Additionally, you'll find content from shows like the Ergo Sigmanauts YouTube channel and Hash it Out W/CW.

Listen to us on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, or add this RSS feed: https://anchor.fm/s/f21c6414/podcast/rss to your preferred podcast app.

Episodes are available to listen to or download (by right-clicking and saving) right on this page.
